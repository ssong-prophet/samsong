MAXAR

It's not every day that
we get to play in space.

But we did when Howard Lance, the CEO of one of the largest, best-in-class, end-to-end advanced satellite and space solutions company, invited us to help them build the brand strategy and identity for Maxar (parent company to MDA, SSL, Digital Globe and Radiant). Satellite technology has never been as profound and critical. It's difficult to avoid a news story today that hasn't used insights from satellite imagery to reveal something hidden, gain access to a remote place or provide insights from that which is hidden to the naked eye. So, we knew the work was important. Not just to us.

For Maxar, the corporate vision we crafted was centered on the belief that breakthrough insights unlock infinite possibilities, and Maxar exists to create the connections and intelligence that power a better world. A powerful purpose that not only is grounded in what they do, but highlights the impact they are striving for.

With that as the creative brief, we were tasked with capturing the head but also the heart of the brand, and translating the power, speed, intelligence and innovation which defined the DNA of the company. Our vision was to create a Living Brand System - one that gave room for the system to adapt to its medium, context and story, and evolve over time.

Design System

Design System

Inspired by the boldness of its purpose, we created a purely black and white visual system. The wordmark embodies strength and the X - the center and pivotal part of the name - becomes the protagonist - both by taking on the shape of the satellite, and in capturing the notion of never-ending speed and innovation in the gradient trail that emerges out of the square. The X becomes an extraction of the breath of capabilities that Maxar delivers between earth and space. The gradient depicts the intrinsic movement of the company - the speed of data, insights and the dimensionality of both the brand and its offerings. (something like this, Jeff?)

The system, like its industry, is kinetic. It is based on a black-and-white x-grid that allows for dynamic storytelling, while always maintaining a sense of systemized order and brand recognition.

The Grid

The Grid

This grid can adapt to any medium, and always integrates the gradient that's derived from the X - bringing a sense of movement and insight to any application, constantly reinforcing critical key elements of the Maxar business.

The system, like its industry, is kinetic. It is based on a black-and-white x-grid that allows for dynamic storytelling, while always maintaining a sense of systemized order and brand recognition.

Given the power of satellite imagery and the significance of the stories that make all Maxar does so relevant and critical, color was left solely for imagery. In this system, the visuals are responsible for bringing color and meaning to the brand, while humanizing technology and focusing on the significance of outcomes.

We hope this Living Brand System will live long, and as far as space and back, to tell the amazing tales of satellite systems, earth imagery, geospatial solutions and breakthrough analytics.
